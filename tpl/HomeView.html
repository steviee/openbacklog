Welcome to OpenBacklog.org

OpenBacklog.org is currently under active development and will be available later this fall.

It aims to be THE platform for open and free feature planning sessions up to whole project planning.

Tweet

What it's about?

With OpenBacklog.org you can coordinate project development in an open manner: Create features with transparent discussion boards and involve your users and customers in the development process of the software they use and love.

Manage your backlog and change priorities of user-stories and epics, let your users and developers vote on functionality and priorities.

Get the Code

The day OpenBacklog.org launches to the public the source will be publicly available for you to fork on github.com.

Take part

OpenBacklog.org will be one of the project backlogs hosted here for everyone to participate and shape the future-features of this site. Stay tuned!
